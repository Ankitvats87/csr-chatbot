### Duration

| Duration                                      |
|-----------------------------------------------|

### Project Activities

| Project Activities                                                                                     |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Identification of plantation sites                                                                   |
| Cleaning, soil testing, and layout designing of the site                                              |
| Pits digging as per layout                                                                            |
| Plantation of saplings at the identified locations                                                    |
| Geo-tagging of plantation site                                                                        |
| Give Me Trees Trust will be responsible for maintenance, Labour, watering, manuring and replacement of sapling in case it is damaged due to any reason (like storm, damage by animal etc.) |
| The complete process will be 100% organic in nature, using dense tree planting style methods with 0% pesticide usage to grow a dense forest. |
| The Give Me Trees Trust team will take full care of the planted saplings for the stipulated project duration of 3 years. After 3 years of planting, the trees become self-sufficient. |
| Give Me Trees Trust will also deploy a field team to keep a constant check on the planted saplings to make sure that they remain healthy. |
| Give Me Trees will provide quarterly/annual reports to PTC India, informing about project progress and the health of the saplings. |
| GMT will also conduct trainings on nature education for employees (online and offline, whatever is your preference), like tree planting, waste management, seed collection and various other environmental trainings. |
| PTC India can also conduct plantation drives as and when required.                                     |
| Give Me Trees will also make free-of-cost documentaries and videos on the project, showcasing how the area has changed before and after planting. |
